# Arduino Controller for Mirror

## Pin Connections

Pin | Signal Name | Description
--- | --- | ---
`0` | `D0` | Digital 0
`1` | `D1` | Digital 1
`2` | `D2` | Digital 2
`3` | `D3` | Digital 3
`5` | `D5` | Digital 4
`4` | `D4` | Digital 5
`6` | `D6` | Digital 6
`7` | `D7` | Digital 7
`8` | `LD` | Load latch to DAC
`9` | `V_CS` | Select vertical DAC
`10` | `H_CS` | Select horizontal DAC
